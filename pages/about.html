About Anna Robert

Anna Robert is a passionate photographer and graphic designer with years of experience. She specializes in both photography and scrapbooking, creating unique and personalized albums for her clients.

Anna's studio offers a creative space for scrapbookers of all levels, where they can relax, enjoy a coffee, and work on their projects. She also offers training, both online and in-person, for beginners and advanced scrapbook enthusiasts.
